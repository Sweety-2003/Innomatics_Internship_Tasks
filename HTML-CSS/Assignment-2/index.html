About Us


At Kaffeehaus, we are dedicated to bringing you the finest coffee experience in a warm and inviting setting. Established in 2025 in the heart of Bhubaneswar, our coffee shop sources premium beans from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring every cup is crafted with care and rich in flavor. More than just a place to enjoy a great brew, Kaffeehaus is a community hub where friends gather, creativity flourishes, and local events come to life. Join us in savoring exceptional coffee and creating memorable moments together
